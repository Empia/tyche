  1.  abstract val mass: MassMap[A]

    The probability mass function.

    The probability mass function. It contains all the possible outcomes and their respective weights. The weights are supposed to:

    1. be nonnegative, and 2. sum up to 1.
    Example:

     |   val mass = Map(true -> 0.5, false -> 0.5)
     | }
